S.T.U.F.F. Thrift Store / DAV - Chapter 8

"Special Treasures of Useful Fashions and Furniture", also know as S.T.U.F.F, is a shopping place with quality inventory & values like you have never experienced before.


S.T.U.F.F Thrift Store has been supporting the community of Mesa since 1999 and contributing to the Resurrection Street Ministry, Inc. (RSM) providing food, services, and work opportunities to Arizona residents in need. 100% of the proceeds from the store are used to support RSMâ€™s Programs and Outreach events.

Donation Program

Donate it all. S.T.U.F.F. Thrift Store accepts all types of reusable items: Almost everything you can imagine is accepted to give to the needy or provide at below normal costs. We also offer a service to transport items that you would like to donate without having to struggle physically getting the items to us


We provide free clothes and other accessible items for citizens who are eligible in any of the following:

  • Senior (65 and over)
  • Veterans who have disabilities or are low income.
  • Homelessness
  • Families in Crisis

Vehicle Donation

Vehicle donations are also accepted by RSM and S.T.U.F.F. Thrift Store. Used vehicles donations in supporting and helping those in need with contribute of vehicle to improve their lives. Vehicles are passed on to provide transportation to people in need.

Online Shopping

Other ways to shop are via Giving Assistant, Offer Up, Facebook Marketing, and other online platforms. You buying through Smile Amazon will help RSM help even more people in need.
