Food Assistance

Resurrection Street Ministry offers food boxes at our facilities in Mesa, Chandler and other off-site distributions and we help other non-profits with food needs. We provide Maricopa residents with nutritional food boxes containing dairy, breads, produce, vegetables, baked goodies, snacks, non-perishable foods and non-food items. If available, residents will also receive meat, prepared meals, deli items, and hygiene items.

Community Food Program

RSM Food Pantry

In 2021, more than 120,000 people - Veterans, Seniors, Homeless, Families and Group Home Residents received more than 2.5 million pounds of food and thousands of non-food items FREE. Donors gave $3,500,000 (FMV) of nutritional food to Arizona people last year. We strive to ensure there is an nutritional value in our food boxes.

Seniors / Veterans Program

RSM hosts the federal CSFP program (Senior Supplemental Food Program). The program provides seniors with a monthly 'dry' goods box and RSM provides a 'Bonus Box' including produce, dairy, bakery and non-perishables. Resurrection Street Ministry provides to all Seniors (over 60). RSM provides for Veterans (under 60) at the same distribution times. APPOINTMENTS are required, Picture ID and Proof of Address. Contact Valerie (405) 386-7799 to register and receive appointment time.
