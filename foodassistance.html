Food Assistance

Resurrection Street Ministry offers food boxes at our facilities in Mesa, Chandler and other off-site distributions and we help other non-profits with food needs. We provide Maricopa residents with nutritional food boxes containing dairy, breads, produce, vegetables, baked goodies, snacks, non-perishable foods and non-food items. If available, residents will also receive meat, prepared meals, deli items, and hygiene items.
