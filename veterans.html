Veteran Programs

RSM provides serves to Veterans in Fountain Hills, Chandler, Mesa, Tempe, Gilbert, and Apache Junction with Non-Emergency transportation to Medical appointments and other Veteran Service Organizations. Email (ResurrectionStreetMinistry@gmail.com) for additional information or to get involved helping those that served us.

Veteran Ride Service

Veteran's transportation services are available in the East Valley for non-emergency medical appointments. Our team of Veterans and caring Volunteers are Blessed to provide for those that cared enough to serve our Country.


By Appointment Only: (480) 615-2799 / ResurrectionStreetMinistry@gmail.com

Funded by: City of Chandler, DAV Chapter 8 Donors and Private Donations.


Equipment provided by: Mesa United Way / DAV (Disabled American Veterans) Chapter 8 / For Our City - Chandler


No tips: Our Volunteer Drivers love to assist Veterans.

Memory Corner

RSM provides a veteran display area within S.T.U.F.F. Thrift Store with non-selling items that are used for exhibition. Think of it as a small museum containing special items symbolizing the memory of our veteran's heroism.
